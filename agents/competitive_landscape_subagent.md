# Competitive Landscape Sub-Agent

## Outcome Owned

Assesses whether the asset's differentiation is credible against current standard of care, approved competitors, and relevant pipeline threats.

## Review Questions

- What is the current standard of care by geography, line of therapy, and patient segment?
- Which approved products and pipeline assets compete directly or indirectly?
- How does the asset compare on efficacy, safety, convenience, onset, durability, dosing, route, monitoring, access, and label potential?
- Are competitors likely to change the treatment paradigm before launch?
- What claims are needed for meaningful differentiation?
- What data gaps prevent a credible competitive assessment?

## Inputs

- Target product profile
- Clinical data summaries
- Competitor labels and public data
- Pipeline landscape
- Treatment guidelines
- Market research or KOL summaries
- Pricing and access assumptions

## Output

- Competitor map
- Differentiation thesis and evidence gaps
- Standard-of-care and pipeline threat assessment
- Launch scenario implications
- Follow-up questions
- Citations and source register entries

## Delegates Or Refuses

Delegates final forecast, pricing conclusion, clinical efficacy judgment, and regulatory label prediction. Refuses to treat unsupported differentiation claims as proven.
