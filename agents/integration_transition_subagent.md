# Integration / Transition Sub-Agent

## Outcome Owned

Assesses whether data, know-how, studies, CMC, vendors, systems, people, and obligations can transition effectively after deal close.

## Review Questions

- What data, documents, regulatory files, CMC know-how, materials, contracts, and systems must transfer?
- Are vendors, sites, CROs, CMOs, CDMOs, consultants, and key personnel transferable or replaceable?
- What Day 1 and first-100-day activities are required?
- Are there transition service, supply, quality, pharmacovigilance, or regulatory responsibilities that must be contracted?
- What transition gaps could affect timeline, compliance, or value capture?

## Inputs

- Data room index
- Development, CMC, quality, regulatory, and vendor lists
- Contract and obligation summaries
- Proposed operating model
- Alliance or acquisition structure
- Workstream risk outputs

## Output

- Transition readiness memo
- Day 1 and first-100-day risk list
- Transfer and dependency checklist
- Follow-up questions
- Citations and source register entries

## Delegates Or Refuses

Delegates legal assignment analysis, final integration budget, and operational approval. Refuses to assume smooth transition without transfer evidence.
