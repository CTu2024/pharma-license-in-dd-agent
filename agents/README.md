# Agent Index

## Operational Mode Owners

- `scouting_triage_agent.md`: Owns and terminates a public or explicitly non-confidential Scouting engagement.
- `primary_diligence_agent.md`: Independently owns a confidential, primary, data-room, or discipline-level diligence engagement and its integrated recommendation.

Scouting does not activate Primary Diligence automatically. A later user request with substantive diligence material begins a separate Primary Diligence engagement.

## Scientific, Clinical, CMC, And Regulatory

- `target_biology_subagent.md`: Reviews disease biology, target validation, mechanistic rationale, and translational confidence.
- `dmpk_adme_subagent.md`: Reviews exposure, ADME, DDI, metabolism, clearance, and species translation.
- `toxicology_subagent.md`: Reviews nonclinical safety, tox package completeness, safety margins, and target organ findings.
- `clinical_evidence_subagent.md`: Reviews clinical study quality, efficacy, safety, endpoints, populations, and evidence gaps.
- `clinical_pharmacology_subagent.md`: Reviews dose rationale, PK/PD, exposure-response, bridging, DDI, and special population evidence.
- `biostatistics_subagent.md`: Reviews statistical design, endpoint handling, multiplicity, missing data, and robustness.
- `cmc_regulatory_subagent.md`: Reviews CMC, quality, regulatory readiness, and next-milestone package completeness.
- `regulatory_pathway_subagent.md`: Reviews agency history, approval path, label risk, required studies, and regional considerations.

## Market, Access, Value, And Transaction Context

- `competitive_landscape_subagent.md`: Reviews standard of care, competitors, pipeline threats, and differentiation.
- `market_access_heor_subagent.md`: Reviews payer evidence, HTA risk, outcomes evidence, and access barriers.
- `pricing_subagent.md`: Reviews price analogs, regional pressure, net price assumptions, and price sensitivity.
- `ip_triage_subagent.md`: Flags patent, exclusivity, ownership, FTO, and encumbrance issues for legal review.
- `legal_contract_subagent.md`: Flags rights, obligations, sublicensing, change-of-control, termination, and contract constraints for counsel.
- `deal_structure_subagent.md`: Reviews license, option, co-development, milestone, royalty, and risk-sharing structures.
- `financial_valuation_subagent.md`: Reviews valuation assumptions, scenarios, sensitivities, risk adjustment, and deal-term implications.
- `alliance_management_subagent.md`: Reviews governance, decision rights, escalation, and operating model risks.
- `integration_transition_subagent.md`: Reviews data, know-how, vendors, CMC, systems, people, and post-deal transfer readiness.
