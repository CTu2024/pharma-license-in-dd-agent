## Analysis

### Findings That Support The Opportunity

- Reported Phase 2 signal and monthly dosing profile may justify continued diligence.
- Regional license structure can be adjusted to allocate risk.

### Findings That Weaken Or Limit The Opportunity

- POS is uncertain without full clinical and statistical review.
- Phase 3 timing and cost may be affected by CMC scale-up and regulatory uncertainty.
- Premium pricing may be overstated.
- IP encumbrance and rights uncertainty could affect net economics.

### Inconsistencies Or Unverified Claims

- Proposed economics appear ahead of the evidence package.
