# CMC Stage Requirements

Use this reference when checking CMC readiness against the asset stage and next milestone.

## Preclinical / IND-Enabling

Expected evidence:

- Route, dosage form, formulation rationale, and candidate selection support.
- Non-GMP and planned GMP manufacturing process description.
- Analytical method status, identity, purity, potency, assay suitability.
- Initial specifications and impurity strategy.
- Stability plan and early stability data.
- Raw material and critical supplier risks.

## Phase 1 / Phase 1b

Expected evidence:

- GMP clinical batch summary, batch records or batch summary, release specifications.
- Analytical method qualification status and reference standards.
- Formulation, container closure, storage, handling, and in-use stability.
- Stability data supporting clinical use.
- Process description, critical process parameters, deviations, and comparability for major changes.
- Clinical supply plan and vendor/CDMO responsibilities.

## Phase 2

Expected evidence:

- Process history, batch success, yield, deviations, and process changes.
- Analytical method qualification or validation plan.
- Impurity, degradation, potency, and control strategy maturity.
- Stability package with enough duration for Phase 3 planning.
- Scale-up plan and comparability strategy if moving to larger scale.
- Specifications and release testing rationale.

## Phase 3 / Registration

Expected evidence:

- Validated or validation-ready analytical methods.
- Process characterization and validation strategy.
- Comparability evidence for scale, site, formulation, or process changes.
- Long-term and accelerated stability package.
- Control strategy, specifications, reference standards, and impurity justification.
- PPQ plan, facility readiness, quality agreements, and inspection readiness evidence.
- Commercial supply and launch inventory assumptions.

## Approved / Commercial

Expected evidence:

- Process validation, continued process verification, quality history.
- Deviation, OOS/OOT, complaints, recalls, CAPAs, and inspection history.
- Commercial supply resilience, second source, raw material security, and capacity.
