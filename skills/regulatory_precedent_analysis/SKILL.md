# Regulatory Precedent Analysis Skill

## Use When

The opportunity depends on whether the proposed regulatory path is plausible.

## Review Method

1. State target indication, geography, stage, proposed claim, and next regulatory milestone.
2. Review original agency correspondence before company summaries when available.
3. Compare proposed endpoints, population, comparator, duration, and safety database to relevant precedent.
4. Identify required studies, unresolved agency concerns, and regional differences.
5. Translate regulatory uncertainty into timeline, cost, label, and deal-term implications.

## Output Fields

- Regulatory claim
- Source or precedent
- Supportive evidence
- Concern or missing requirement
- Decision impact
- Company request

## Quality Boundary

Do not guarantee agency outcomes. Treat company-prepared agency summaries as secondary until original correspondence is reviewed.
