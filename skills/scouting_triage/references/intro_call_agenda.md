# Intro Call Agenda

Use this reference to generate a scouting-stage intro-call agenda.

## 30-45 Minute Agenda

1. **Asset and transaction snapshot**: asset, target, modality, stage, indications, rights available.
2. **Why now**: why the company is seeking a partner at this stage.
3. **Lead indication logic**: proposed lead indication, alternatives considered, first PoC path.
4. **Reason-to-believe**: strongest evidence point and weakest link.
5. **Data package status**: which reports are complete and available after CDA.
6. **Differentiation**: nearest competitor, standard of care, what must be true to win.
7. **Development path**: next milestone, critical path, key risks, partner capabilities needed.
8. **IP and control**: ownership, rights, encumbrances, patent family summary.
9. **Process**: timeline, competitive process, desired structure, decision dates.
10. **Next step**: non-confidential follow-up, CDA, or pass/monitor.

## Call Output

After the call, update:

- Scouting recommendation.
- Unverified claims.
- CDA gate questions.
- Internal alignment questions.
- Walk-away signals.
