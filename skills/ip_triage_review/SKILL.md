# IP Triage Review Skill

## Use When

Exclusivity, patent coverage, FTO, ownership, or encumbrance issues could affect deal value or rights.

## Review Method

1. Map patent families by subject: composition, method, formulation, manufacturing, biomarkers, combinations.
2. Identify owner, license chain, territory, status, expiry, extensions, and maintenance concerns.
3. Flag FTO, opposition, IPR, litigation, inventorship, assignment, and royalty stack issues.
4. Identify regulatory exclusivity opportunities or gaps.
5. Convert triage flags into counsel review requests.

## Output Fields

- IP issue
- Patent / agreement / source
- Business impact
- Legal review needed
- Severity
- Company request

## Quality Boundary

This skill produces issue spotting for counsel, not legal advice, claim construction, enforceability opinions, or FTO opinions.
