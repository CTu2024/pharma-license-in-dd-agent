# Market Access / HEOR Gap Skill

## Use When

Access, reimbursement, or payer evidence requirements could materially affect value.

## Review Method

1. Define target markets, patient segments, standard of care, and expected launch setting.
2. Identify payer-relevant evidence: comparator, endpoints, durability, quality of life, resource use, and PROs.
3. Compare current evidence package against likely HTA and payer expectations.
4. Identify access barriers: restrictions, step edits, evidence gaps, budget impact, and price pressure.
5. Convert gaps into evidence-generation needs and valuation implications.

## Output Fields

- Access claim
- Evidence available
- Payer / HTA requirement
- Gap
- Impact on forecast or pricing
- Follow-up request

## Quality Boundary

Do not assume access from regulatory approval alone. Access claims need payer-relevant evidence and market-specific context.
