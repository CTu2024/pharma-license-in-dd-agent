# Differentiation Scorecard

Use this reference to rate whether a scouting opportunity has enough competitive logic to justify CDA.

## Differentiation Dimensions

Rate each as `Advantage / Parity / Disadvantage / Unknown`.

- Efficacy.
- Safety / tolerability.
- Durability.
- Dosing interval.
- Route and administration burden.
- Onset of action.
- Biomarker or patient selection.
- Label potential.
- Combination potential.
- CMC / supply practicality.
- Access and payer relevance.
- IP/exclusivity.

## What Must Be True To Win

For each opportunity, write 3-5 "must be true" statements:

- Must show clinically meaningful benefit in the lead indication.
- Must match or exceed competitor safety profile.
- Must validate dosing convenience in humans.
- Must have enough IP/control to support deal value.
- Must reach PoC before competitor landscape closes.

## CDA Gate Questions

- Who is the asset trying to beat first?
- What competitor evidence is strongest?
- What claim is most likely to fail?
- If the headline differentiation fails, is there a second way to win?
- What readout or report after CDA would most change the competitive view?
