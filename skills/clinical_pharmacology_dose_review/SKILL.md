# Clinical Pharmacology Dose Review Skill

## Use When

The diligence question depends on whether dose and exposure are defensible for the next study or filing.

## Review Method

1. State the proposed dose, regimen, population, and next milestone.
2. Review PK, PD, target engagement, biomarker, efficacy, and safety evidence.
3. Assess exposure-response for benefit and risk.
4. Identify bridging needs across formulation, geography, population, or phase.
5. Flag DDI, food effect, renal, hepatic, QT, and special population gaps.

## Output Fields

- Dose claim
- Evidence supporting dose
- Evidence against or limiting dose
- Missing clinical pharmacology work
- Development implication
- Company request

## Quality Boundary

Do not endorse dose selection based only on tolerability or selected efficacy charts. Require traceable PK/PD and exposure-response support.
