# Transition Planning Skill

## Use When

The deal requires transfer or operational handoff after signing or close.

## Review Method

1. Identify what must transfer: documents, data, regulatory files, CMC know-how, materials, contracts, vendors, people, systems.
2. Separate Day 1, first 100 days, and longer-term transition needs.
3. Flag dependencies: consents, vendor assignments, quality agreements, supply obligations, pharmacovigilance, data standards, and key personnel.
4. Identify transition service needs and ownership.
5. Convert gaps into closing conditions, covenants, or operating plans.

## Output Fields

- Transfer item
- Current owner
- Required evidence
- Gap or dependency
- Timeline impact
- Company request

## Quality Boundary

Do not assume transferability. Require evidence for vendor, data, regulatory, CMC, quality, and material handoffs.
