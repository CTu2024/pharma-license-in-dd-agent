# Pricing Analog Review Skill

## Use When

Pricing assumptions materially affect valuation or deal terms.

## Review Method

1. State proposed price, geography, population, duration, and treatment setting.
2. Identify analogs and explain why each is comparable or not comparable.
3. Check gross-to-net, rebate, HTA, reference pricing, and access restriction assumptions.
4. Build low/base/high price ranges tied to evidence and differentiation.
5. Flag where pricing depends on unproven label, endpoint, or access assumptions.

## Output Fields

- Pricing assumption
- Analog or evidence
- Adjustment rationale
- Low/base/high range
- Sensitivity implication
- Follow-up request

## Quality Boundary

Do not accept a price analog unless indication, setting, benefit, route, duration, access environment, and launch context are reasonably comparable.
