# First-In-Human Safety Margin Questions

Use this reference when deciding whether tox information supports CDA or deeper diligence.

## CDA Gate Questions

- What is the proposed FIH starting dose?
- Which method supports it: NOAEL, HNSTD, MABEL, pharmacologically active dose, or hybrid approach?
- What species is most relevant and why?
- What exposure margin exists at proposed starting and target doses?
- Were findings reversible, monitorable, and clinically manageable?
- Are there class effects or target biology concerns?
- What clinical stopping rules and monitoring are proposed?

## Safety Margin Interpretation

- `Encouraging`: NOAEL/HNSTD, TK, recovery, and monitoring are coherent.
- `Conditional`: Tox looks acceptable but margin, ADA, recovery, or species relevance need review.
- `Concerning`: Target organ, immune, hematology, CRS, or irreversible findings may limit FIH.
- `Not assessable`: Full tox/TK package missing.

## Scouting Output

At scouting stage, write:

- What is claimed.
- Why it matters for FIH.
- What is missing before CDA.
- What must be verified after CDA.
