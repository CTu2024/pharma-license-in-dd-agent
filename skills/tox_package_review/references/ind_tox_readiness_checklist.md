# IND Toxicology Readiness Checklist

Use this reference for IND-enabling or pre-FIH assets.

## Core Evidence

- GLP tox reports in relevant species.
- Study duration, route, dose levels, recovery, and toxicokinetics.
- NOAEL or HNSTD rationale, depending on modality and region.
- Safety pharmacology package.
- Genotox/reprotox/carcinogenicity plan as relevant.
- Cytokine release, hemolysis, immunogenicity, local tolerance for biologics as relevant.
- Target tissue expression and on-target/off-tissue risk.
- FIH starting dose rationale: NOAEL/HNSTD/MABEL and exposure margin.
- Clinical monitoring implications.

## Scouting Questions Before CDA

- Which GLP tox studies are complete and final?
- What is the NOAEL/HNSTD and in which species?
- Were any target organ, immune, hematology, injection-site, or recovery findings observed?
- Are TK and ADA available?
- What safety pharmacology or biologic-specific assays are complete?
- What is the planned FIH starting dose and safety margin?

## Red Flags

- NOAEL is quoted without TK or margin.
- Tox study species relevance is unclear.
- Immune findings are dismissed without context.
- ADA complicates exposure interpretation.
- Local tolerance or CRS risk is not addressed for antibody/biologic.
- Recovery findings are incomplete.
