# Toxicology Package Review Skill

## Use When

Nonclinical safety evidence may affect clinical progression, regulatory readiness, or deal risk.

## Review Method

1. Identify stage, dosing duration, route, species, and next clinical milestone.
2. Compare provided tox package against stage-appropriate expectations.
3. Extract target organs, severity, reversibility, monitorability, and exposure relationship.
4. Check genotox, reprotox, carcinogenicity, safety pharmacology, and class risks as relevant.
5. Translate tox findings into clinical monitoring or development implications.

## Output Fields

- Study or missing requirement
- Finding or gap
- Exposure / margin context
- Human relevance
- Severity and likelihood
- Required follow-up

## Quality Boundary

Do not call a tox issue acceptable without exposure, reversibility, monitorability, and stage-appropriate study context.
