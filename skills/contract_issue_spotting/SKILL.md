# Contract Issue Spotting Skill

## Use When

Existing or proposed contract terms may constrain the license-in transaction or post-deal operation.

## Review Method

1. Identify proposed rights: product, field, territory, indication, modality, and exclusivity.
2. Review obligations: milestones, royalties, diligence, development, supply, reporting, audit, and governance.
3. Flag constraints: sublicensing, assignment, change of control, termination, publication, data use, consent, and third-party rights.
4. Identify inconsistencies between business assumptions and contract rights.
5. Convert issues into counsel questions and deal-team decision points.

## Output Fields

- Contract issue
- Clause or source
- Business assumption affected
- Risk / constraint
- Counsel question
- Deal-team decision needed

## Quality Boundary

This skill identifies issues for counsel. It does not provide legal advice, contract interpretation, or drafting positions.
