# Deal Structure Review Skill

## Use When

The transaction structure should reflect diligence risk, control needs, and valuation uncertainty.

## Review Method

1. State proposed structure and rights scope.
2. Map major diligence risks to economic and control levers.
3. Consider staged structures: option, milestone gating, territory split, indication split, cost share, or co-development.
4. Identify terms affected by missing data or unresolved risks.
5. Convert risks into negotiation issues and conditions precedent.

## Output Fields

- Deal issue
- Related risk or assumption
- Structure option
- Economic implication
- Control / governance implication
- Negotiation question

## Quality Boundary

Do not recommend economics detached from risk, rights, and valuation assumptions. Escalate legal drafting and final negotiation authority.
