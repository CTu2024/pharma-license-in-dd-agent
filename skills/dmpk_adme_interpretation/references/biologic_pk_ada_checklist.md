# Biologic PK / ADA Checklist

Use this reference for antibody or biologic assets where half-life, exposure, ADA, or dosing convenience is a key scouting claim.

## Required Evidence After CDA

- Species PK reports, especially NHP when relevant.
- Bioanalytical method validation or qualification.
- ADA assay status and immunogenicity results.
- FcRn binding or half-life engineering package if claimed.
- Dose proportionality and exposure variability.
- Target-mediated drug disposition assessment.
- Tissue distribution or target expression relevance when needed.
- PK/PD or target engagement relationship.
- Human dose projection and FIH starting dose rationale.

## Scouting Questions Before CDA

- Which species support the half-life claim?
- Is the comparator molecule sourced commercially, internally made, or surrogate?
- Is ADA observed in NHP or other species?
- Is Q4W/Q8W/Q12W dosing a modeled hypothesis or directly supported?
- What clinical dosing interval is proposed for Phase 1 and why?
- What data will be available after CDA to validate dosing convenience?

## Red Flags

- Dosing convenience is a headline claim but PK report is not final.
- Half-life comparison uses internally synthesized competitor without clear comparability.
- ADA data are absent or minimized.
- NHP PK is extrapolated directly to human dosing without model detail.
- Exposure is not linked to pharmacology or tox margins.
